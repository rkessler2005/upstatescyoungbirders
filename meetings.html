Clemson Botanical Garden Bird Walk

Morning walk through the Botanical Garden with the Winged Wednesday group. Participants practiced bird identification, photography, and observation skills.

Species observed included:

  • Carolina Wren
  • Northern Mockingbird
  • Eastern Bluebird
  • Red-bellied Woodpecker
  • Canada Goose
